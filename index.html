Guidelines
How to use & assumptions (Tax Year 2026)
How to use
  • Enter either Monthly Salary or Annual Salary (only one).
  • If using Monthly Salary, optionally select Start/End dates for partial-year employment.
  • If using Annual Salary, Start/End dates are locked and schedule is split evenly across 12 months.
  • Click Calculate to see total tax and monthly allocation schedule.
  • Use Download PDF/CSV for HR sharing and employee record.
Assumptions
  • Based on FBR Salary slabs (Section 149) for Tax Year 2026.
  • Partial months are calculated using day-based fractions (monthly mode).
  • Tax allocation is for transparency; actual payroll withholding may vary by employer policy.
  • Rounding differences may occur in allocation.
Not included
  • Exemptions, allowances, rebates, other income, credits, deductions, special cases.
  • Any employer-specific payroll adjustments or non-standard benefits.
Estimate only — refer to official FBR law/notifications for final position. No liability assumed.
